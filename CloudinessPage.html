Cloudiness

The relationship between cloudiness and latitude is also weak. It is important to note that there are very large groupings of cities that have around 0%, 75%, or 90% cloudiness. It appears that the majority of cities fall into one of these categories, with 0% cloudiness having by far the most cities. There are some other weak groupings of cities at 20% and 40% cloudiness.
